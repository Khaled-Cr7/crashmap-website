Car Safety Check

Answer a few quick questions about your vehicle and yourself before a drive. You'll get an instant Critical / High / Low roadworthiness rating with a recommendation — takes under a minute.

1. What's the condition of your brakes?
2. What's the condition of your tyres?
3. Are your lights working? (headlights, brake lights, indicators)
4. When was your last service?
5. Have you had at least 8 hours of sleep?
6. Have you taken any medication that may cause drowsiness? (e.g. flu medication)

Please answer every question before submitting.
